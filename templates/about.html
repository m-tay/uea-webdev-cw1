Horse Riding (20 minutes)

You can ride horses from our on-site stable into the nearby countryside. A great way to spend a day getting close to nature.

We have a great rail connection to London. Book a first class trip with us for some retail therapy or to catch and show.

If you want to spend a day relaxing on the beach, we can take you there! Clear water and quiet surroundings await you.
